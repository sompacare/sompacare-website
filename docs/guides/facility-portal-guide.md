# Facility Portal — User Guide

**URL:** https://facility.yourdomain.com (local: http://localhost:3002)

For facility managers and schedulers to post shifts, review applicants, approve timecards, and manage payroll.

---

## Getting started

1. Sign in with your facility account
2. Confirm your facility and locations are set up
3. Post your first shift

**Mobile app:** Sompacare Facility app for on-the-go applicant review and timecard approval.

---

## Home dashboard

Quick view of:

- Published vs draft shifts
- Pending applicants
- Timecards awaiting approval
- Payroll anomalies (AI alerts)
- Unread notifications

---

## Managing shifts

**My Shifts**

| Action | Steps |
|--------|-------|
| Create shift | Fill title, role, rate, times, location, slots |
| Save draft | Shift stays internal until published |
| Publish | Shift goes live — nurses are notified |
| View applicants | Tap shift → see AI-ranked applicants |
| Cancel | Remove shift (reason required if assignments exist) |

**AI match scores** help you prioritize applicants by compliance, reliability, and role fit.

---

## Applicants

**Applicants tab**

1. Review pending applications with match scores
2. View worker compliance status before approving
3. **Approve** → creates assignment (worker must confirm)
4. **Reject** → worker notified with reason

---

## Staff schedule & timecards

**Schedule tab**

- See confirmed assignments by date
- Monitor clock-in/out status

**Timecard approval**

1. Worker clocks out → timecard submitted automatically
2. Review hours, GPS verification, and gross amount
3. **Approve** → triggers invoice generation
4. Dispute workflow (if hours look incorrect)

---

## Payroll & billing

**Payroll tab**

- Generate pay runs from approved timecards
- Review overtime calculations
- Approve and process payouts
- Export CSV for accounting

**Invoices tab**

- View auto-generated invoices per approved timecard
- Pay via Stripe (facility billing)

---

## Compliance queue

Review pending license and certification verifications for workers at your facility. Approve or reject with notes.

Run **Scan expirations** to trigger alerts for credentials expiring in 30/14/7 days.

---

## Notifications

Real-time updates for:

- New applications
- Worker confirmed assignment
- Timecard submitted
- Payroll anomalies detected

---

## Support

For platform issues, contact Sompacare support. For worker disputes, use the assignment and timecard notes fields for audit trail.

**Tips:**
- Publish shifts 48+ hours ahead for better fill rates
- Use AI match scores to fill urgent shifts faster
- Approve timecards within 24h to keep workers paid on schedule
